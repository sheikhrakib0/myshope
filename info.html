What Happens When You Click the Button?

When you click the "Login" button, you will be redirected to the login page where you can enter your credentials.

Once your credentials are verified (in a real implementation), you will be logged in and redirected to your dashboard or home page.

For the "Sign Up" button, you'll be redirected to the sign-up page, where you can create a new account by entering your email, full name, and password.

For more information on the actions, please visit our FAQ page or contact support.
